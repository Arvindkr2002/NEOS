AIDE acts as an assistive diagnostic tool for radiologists. AIDE accurately detects and highlights suspected lesions in a scan. AIDE detects multiple abnormalities in a Chest-CT such as Ground-glass opacities, Consolidation, Crazy paving pattern, Fibrosis, and Nodules in less than 5 minutes.
AIDE quantifies the severity of the condition based on the volume of the lung parenchyma and infected lesions. AIDE also assigns a severity score which can be further used to monitor the progression of the patient.
AIDE provides an assistive reporting tool with a preliminary report and structured reporting proformas that allow the radiologist to report with ease while diagnosing the patient
