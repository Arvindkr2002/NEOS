Smart-worklist that allows the radiologist to access studies irrespective of the geography or device they are using. FLOW understands working behaviors and efficiently allocates studies to the most suitable radiologist to maximise diagnostic productivity. Triaging tools allow the radiologist to provide immediate attention and prioritize severe conditions over regular screening procedures.
Seamless communication channel that allows peer-to-peer sharing of studies and reports within the radiologists’ network. This allows radiologists to share their findings with their peers to get second opinions.
Analytics dashboard enables the radiologist and administration to derive suitable insights to monitor their diagnostic performance. These metrics can be used to enhance diagnostic productivity.
